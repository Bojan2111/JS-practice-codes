Fibonacci sequence

Here are three different methods of calculating Fibonacci sequence. Enter the number and press the corresponding button to see the result.

This method will calculate the requested length of sequence. E.g. if you input number 8, the result will display 8 numbers in Fibonacci sequence.

This method will show the sequence between 0 and requested number, e.g. numbers from 0 to 1000, just enter 1000 and click the button to see the result.

Third method shows the n-th number in Fibonacci sequence. Just enter the number you want. For example, the 9th numbre in sequence is 21. Try it now!
